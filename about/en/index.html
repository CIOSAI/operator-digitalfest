Operator Digitalfest and its predecessor Shorthair Newmedia Meetup aim to foster a demoscene community in Taiwan. Demoscene started in the Nordics in the 80s, when cracking groups would put their logos or title sequence in the games/softwares they cracked, this slowly escalated into short animations, sometimes with music, or even snarky references to other cracking groups. As it gets more and more elaborate, some have forgone cracking and focused on showing off their cool "demos" -executable files that create dazzling graphics and music when ran. The modern demoscene often attracts DJ/VJ, technical artists, 3D/SFX engineers, live coders and many more, and its retro root thrives with pixelart, tracker music, and oldskool demo.

Shorthair Discord server was founded by CIOSAI to promote Demoscene culture in Taiwan, mostly for event announcements, but we'd also love to hear about your works :)
