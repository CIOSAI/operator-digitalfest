Compo Rules

  • No plagiarism or violation of copyrights, if you use any third party assets please pay attention to the license
  • No repeated entries, meaning the exact same work must not have been shown to the public in exhibits or social medias. private groupchats are fine
  • If your work is found by organizers to include NSFW, hate speech, or discrimination contents, it will be disqualified. Please do contact us if you have doubts about it.
  • By submitting we assume you grant people rights to distribute your works on Scene.org, Demozoo, and Pouet for archiving (you may opt out, remember to tick the box when you submit)
  • By submitting we assume you grant the organizers rights to promote Shorthair community and other future events (you may opt out, remember to tick the box when you submit)
  • Please provide and opensource your project files and/or source code, or any necessary resource to recreate your work. Take a look at license like Creative Commons or MIT! (You don't have to allow commercial use) You'll find the upload slot on the submission form.
  • Please take 10 minute as the general time limit, if the duration of your piece can't be easily defined please talk to CIOSAI.
